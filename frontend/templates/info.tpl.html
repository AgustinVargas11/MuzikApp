Muzik

Muzik encourages people to listen to new music and explore new genres.


After a few searches Muzik will suggest new songs within the same genre for you to listen to.


If you're feeling adventurous, turn off the same genre suggestions in your profile page. Muzik will then only suggest new songs from a different genre.


Worried about how we're using your email address? Check out our privacy statement. I think you'll find it pretty straightforward and direct.
